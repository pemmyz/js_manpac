CONTROLS

FEATURES

  • 1 Player & 2 Player Shared Screen
  • Classic Ghost AI (Scatter, Chase, Frightened)
  • Procedural 3D Maze Generation
  • Synthesized Web Audio

GAMEPLAY

Eat all pellets to advance. Avoid ghosts unless they are blue (Frightened). Power pellets turn ghosts vulnerable for a short time.

In 2-player mode, ghosts hunt the nearest player.

ABOUT

A browser-based 3D arcade game built with Three.js (ES Modules).

Tech: Three.js r160, Web Audio API.
